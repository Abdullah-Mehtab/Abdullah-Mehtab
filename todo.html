The Never-Ending List

by Abdullah Mehtab

You might be wondering what an the never-ending list is and why it is here. In contrast with a bucket list, an the never-ending list is ever evolving and is expected to grow with time: you might cross things off the list, but you are also always adding new items to the list. It is, therefore, the never-ending to finish every single item in the list, hence the name.

I'm keeping my list public to encourage myself to update it and to get other people to start their very own lists as well.
